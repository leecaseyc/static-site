China

In 2009 I had an amazing oppurtunity to be a student ambassador for People to People, and traveled with them to China. On this trip I visted some well known cities such as; Shanghai, Beijing, and Shaoxing while also staying a night with a lovely family in Xi'an. This was my first experience in a foreign country as well as my first time traveling without my parents. The trip changed the way I viewed the world, both at home and abroad. I grew a deeper appreciation for a culture I have always found myself fascinated with for as long as I can remember.
